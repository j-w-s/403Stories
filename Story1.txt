He drank to remember the pain and wrote to forget her name. But in writing, all he could write was the instructions to a LEGO monster truck guide dedicated to her image. It was a peculiar way of coping, a bizarre form of therapy that seemed to take on a life of its own. As he meticulously crafted each page of the LEGO monster truck guide, he found himself lost in the world of plastic bricks and tiny mechanical parts. The process became a kind of meditation, a distraction from the haunting memories that had led him to this point. Then, the brickster arrived...
